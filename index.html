Jonny Vs Dinosaur

Emerging from the urban sprawl of North-West London, Jonny Vs Dinosaur meshes delicate acoustic ballads with hard rocking guitar melodies to form something truly their own. From rehearsing and recording in bedrooms, warehouses and suspiciously smelling rehearsal rooms to performing in retro ballrooms, underground bars and the odd local pub across London, these guys are all about the music!
