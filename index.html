About Me

I'm a museum professional with ten years of collection management experience and a strong background in practical object handling and care.

I am passionate about using digital technology to streamline and improve collection processes and have created a number of bespoke software to aid with storage and object packing design.

First off I moved to Melbourne, Australia! Due to visa restrictions I decided to dive into software development and programming. I completed courses such as CS50x, CS50p, CS50sql and currently working through CS50web.

I also continued to support my puzzle game Super Puzzle Sudoku as well as designing filmaculategrid.com, a daily film trivia game.

British Museum 2017-2023
